        About DexyBot

        DexyBot is a tradingbot and is designed specifically for NightTrader.exchange, a decentralized exchange platform for Bitcoin. It enables users to automate their trading activities on this exchange. With a focus on Bitcoin trading, the bot allows users to configure various parameters and settings to optimize their trading strategies.

        Using market making techniques, the tradingbot creates liquidity by placing buy and sell orders on NightTrader.exchange. By capitalizing on the spread between bid and ask prices, the bot aims to generate profits. It supports a range of trading pairs and offers the flexibility to trade across different time intervals.

        The bot is equipped with advanced features such as order cancellation, order placement, order matching, and error handling. It can adapt to changing market conditions and handle various scenarios effectively. Additionally, users have the ability to customize the bot's behavior by attaching event handlers to events such as start, stop, interval change, order cancellation, order placement, order matching, empty balance, and error events.

        To facilitate trading, the tradingbot fetches market data from NightTrader.exchange, retrieves account balances, and utilizes random number generation for determining optimal wait times. It provides a straightforward interface to start and stop the trading process, making it user-friendly and accessible.

        Overall, the DexyBot tradingbot offers a powerful and automated solution for trading Bitcoin on NightTrader.exchange. By leveraging its features and customization options, users can enhance their trading experience and potentially improve their profitability on the platform.
